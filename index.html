About me

I am a design and multimedia student, a technology and design lover. As long as I can remember, I have been working in these two areas and today I am happy to have the opportunity not to have to choose between them.

AMBITIONS

I have the same need that design has for technology, I don't feel completely satisfied with creating this nice idea if I can't bring it to life. So in the near future, I want to be a full stack web developer. I chose web because there are so many nice ideas out there on this big platform that inspire me a lot and a big platform always come with a lot of opportunities.
